FAQ

Some of the frequently asked questions

1.

What services does Combo Square offer?

Combo Square offers a wide range of services including content creation, reels editing and creation, advertisement creatives, ad video creatives, logo designing, branding logos, web designing and development, UI/UX designing, portfolio services, landing page services, and product & packaging design.

2.

What is the pricing for your services?

Our pricing varies based on the service provided. For example, content creation and reels editing range from ₹800-1k, logo designing is priced at ₹1.5k-2k, and website designing and development costs between ₹8k-10k. For a detailed pricing list, please refer to our services page or contact us directly

3.

How long does it take to complete a project?

The timeline for project completion depends on the scope and complexity of the project. Typically, smaller projects like logo design or ad creatives can be completed within a week, whereas larger projects like website development or comprehensive branding may take several weeks. We ensure timely delivery and keep our clients informed throughout the process

4.

Can you customize your services to fit our specific needs?

Absolutely! At Combo Square, we understand that each client has unique requirements. We offer customizable solutions tailored to meet your specific needs and goals. Whether it's a bespoke branding package or a unique web design, we work closely with you to achieve the best results

5.

What makes Combo Square different from other designing agencies?

Combo Square stands out due to our innovative approach, client-centric philosophy, and commitment to using the latest technologies. Our diverse range of services, expert team, and dedication to delivering high-quality, creative solutions ensure that we consistently exceed client expectations. Our tagline, "We Elevate Your Brand by Creativity," encapsulates our mission to enhance your brand's presence and impact
